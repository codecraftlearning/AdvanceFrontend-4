Tree

A tree is a perennial plant with a strong trunk, branches, and leaves that grows over many years. It plays a critical role in maintaining ecological balance by absorbing carbon dioxide and releasing oxygen, making the air cleaner and healthier.

Cars

A car is a motor vehicle designed for transportation, typically used to carry passengers on roads. It operates using an internal combustion engine or an electric motor, converting energy into motion. Cars provide convenience, speed, and flexibility compared to other modes of transport, making them an essential part of daily life.
