Italian Food

Italian cuisine is a diverse Mediterranean tradition centered on fresh, regional ingredients like tomatoes, olive oil, pasta, cheese, and cured meats. Key staples include varied pasta shapes (e.g., carbonara, ravioli), Neapolitan pizza, risotto, and desserts like tiramisu. Meals often follow a structured, multi-course format, from antipasti to dolce
